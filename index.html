Celebrate the spirit of innovation and engineering excellence on Engineers' Day, observed annually on September 15th in honor of the legendary Sir M. Visvesvaraya. This day is a tribute to the remarkable contributions of engineers who have shaped modern India through their ingenuity and dedication. As we commemorate this occasion, we also aim to inspire and empower the next generation of engineers, encouraging them to continue building a better and more sustainable future. Join us in recognizing the pivotal role that engineers play in transforming ideas into reality and driving the nation's progress."
